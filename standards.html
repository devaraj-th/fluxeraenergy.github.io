Battery Standards & Certification Support

Comprehensive testing, validation, and certification preparation for lithium-ion cells, EV battery packs, and grid-scale energy storage systems aligned with national and international standards.

Certification Readiness Across the Battery Value Chain

Fluxera provides end-to-end support for battery safety testing and compliance documentation aligned to Indian and international standards.

From lithium-ion cells to complete battery energy storage systems, our testing infrastructure and certification readiness analytics help manufacturers, OEMs, and system integrators meet safety and performance standards efficiently.

Cell-Level Standards

Safety and performance validation for lithium-ion battery cells.

Cell-level testing is the foundation of battery safety. Fluxera supports comprehensive validation aligned to national and international cell safety standards, covering electrical performance, thermal behavior, mechanical integrity, and abuse testing.

Supported Cell Standards

  • BIS IS 16046: Indian standard for lithium-ion cells
  • IEC 62133: International safety standard for portable batteries
  • IEC 62619: Safety requirements for secondary lithium cells and batteries
  • UN38.3: Transport safety testing for lithium batteries
  • UL 1642: Safety standard for lithium batteries

Testing Scope: Capacity verification, internal resistance, cycle life, thermal runaway propagation, vibration, shock, altitude simulation.

EV Battery Pack Standards

  • AIS-156: Indian standard for EV battery systems (mandatory for Indian market)
  • AIS-038: Battery operated vehicles requirements
  • UL 2580: Safety standard for EV batteries
  • ISO 12405: Electrically propelled road vehicles - Test specification for lithium-ion traction battery packs
  • UNECE R100: European regulation for EV battery systems

Certification Time: 4-6 weeks (industry average: 6-12 months)

EV Battery Pack Certification

Fast-track AIS-156 and international EV battery pack compliance.

EV manufacturers face 6-12 month certification backlogs that delay product launches. Fluxera's testing intelligence platform reduces certification time to 4-6 weeks while providing comprehensive safety validation.

Our testing covers thermal management, BMS validation, mechanical integrity, electrical safety, and complete abuse testing aligned to AIS-156, UL 2580, and ISO 12405 requirements.

BESS System Certification

Grid-scale and commercial battery energy storage system safety validation.

Battery energy storage systems require comprehensive system-level safety testing to secure project financing and insurance coverage. Fluxera provides complete testing and certification support aligned to IEC, UL, and CEA standards.

Our system-level testing includes fire propagation analysis, thermal management validation, grid integration compliance, and complete electrical and mechanical safety verification.

BESS Standards

  • IEC 62933: Electrical energy storage systems - Safety requirements
  • UL 9540: Safety standard for energy storage systems
  • UL 9540A: Fire safety testing for energy storage systems
  • IEC 62620: Secondary cells and batteries for industrial applications
  • CEA Grid Standards: Indian grid integration requirements

Applications: Grid-scale storage, C&I installations, renewable integration, peak shaving, frequency regulation.

Transport Safety Compliance

UN38.3 testing and documentation for lithium battery transport.

Lithium batteries must meet UN38.3 safety requirements for air, sea, and ground transport. Fluxera provides complete UN38.3 testing services and MSDS documentation support for battery manufacturers and logistics providers.

Our transport compliance testing includes altitude simulation, thermal testing, vibration, shock, external short circuit, impact, overcharge, and forced discharge tests as mandated by IATA, IMDG, and ADR regulations.

Transport Compliance

  • Vibration and shock testing
