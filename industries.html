Industries We Serve

Fluxera provides intelligence infrastructure across the battery energy value chain—from manufacturers to operators to financial institutions.

EV Manufacturers

Fast-track certification and safety validation for electric vehicle battery systems.

EV manufacturers face 6-12 month certification backlogs that delay product launches and tie up working capital. Fluxera's testing intelligence platform reduces certification time to 4-6 weeks while providing comprehensive safety validation aligned to AIS-156, BIS 16046, and IEC standards.

Our digital twin and AI health analytics enable continuous monitoring of deployed vehicle fleets, predictive maintenance scheduling, and warranty optimization.

Battery Manufacturers

Comprehensive testing and validation infrastructure for battery cell and module producers.

Battery manufacturers need independent validation to qualify products for OEM integration and export markets. Fluxera provides complete characterization services covering electrical performance, thermal behavior, mechanical integrity, and safety compliance.

Our testing data feeds into digital twin models that enable predictive quality control and process optimization.

BESS Developers

Safety certification and risk intelligence for battery energy storage system deployments.

Grid-scale and C&I battery storage projects require comprehensive safety validation to secure project financing and insurance coverage. Fluxera provides system-level testing aligned to IEC 62619, UL 9540A, and NFPA 855 standards.

Our risk intelligence layer generates safety reports and performance predictions that satisfy lender due diligence requirements and enable lower insurance premiums.
