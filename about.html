About Fluxera Energy

Infrastructure company building safe and intelligent systems for battery energy storage. Founded in 2026 and headquartered in Tumakuru, Karnataka, India.

What We Do

Fluxera provides integrated infrastructure for battery energy storage systems across the complete project lifecycle. We combine energy storage intelligence, compliance infrastructure, and physical safety systems into unified solutions for utilities, EPC companies, and industrial operators.

Our infrastructure spans pre-deployment intelligence reports, physical battery testing coordination, certification support, and operational safety systems installation.

How We Work

We operate as an integrated infrastructure company, not a consulting firm. Our solutions combine data intelligence, physical validation, and engineering-grade infrastructure delivery.

From pre-bid intelligence reports to post-deployment safety infrastructure installation, we provide end-to-end support for energy storage projects.

Trust Through Validation

Physical testing and certification infrastructure builds stakeholder confidence in project safety and quality.

Engineering-Grade Delivery

Physical infrastructure solutions designed, supplied, and installed to industrial standards.
